How To Apply For UET Peshawar For online Admission

  1. Deposit cash Rs.2700/- only if you want to apply on both Open Merit and Non-Subsidized Scheme. OR
  2. Deposit cash Rs.2500/- only if you want to apply on Open Merit Basis OR
  3. Deposit cash Rs.200/- only if you want to apply on Non-Subsidized Scheme.
  4. Visit www.enggentrancetest.pk and type Bank SEQ No., Date, Branch Code, Your Entry Test Roll No., and your subject group (i.e. Pre-Engg or Computer Sc. or DAE )
  5. After this, in STEP-1, update your academic records as you provided during ETEA Registration.
  6. In STEP-2, upload the scan copies of all the required documents as per advertisement.
  7. In STEP-3, complete your order of Preferences/disciplines.
  8. In STEP-4, review and confirm your data (i.e. Academic records and Choices of Disciplines)
  9. Finally, click on SUBMIT button to submit your application online.
  10. You will get a filled form of your data, keep it with yourself. Bring this form at the time of interview for admission alongwith your original documents.

    11. Required Documents to be Uploaded:

  11. S.S.C DMC/O-Level Equivalence
  12. Intermediate Part-I/DAE Part-I / A-Level Equivalence
  13. Intermediate Part-II / DAE Part-II / A-Level Equivalence
  14. DAE Part-III DMC
  15. Additional Maths DMC (if any)
  16. Domicile Certificate [Deposit registration processing fee in any branch of UBL (in UET Account No. 205505522) throughout Pakistan.]
